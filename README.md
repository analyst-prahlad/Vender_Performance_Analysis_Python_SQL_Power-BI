# 📊 Vendor Performance Analysis

**SQL | Python | Power BI | Business Intelligence | Data Analytics**

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes over **10,000+ vendor transactions** to evaluate sales performance, purchasing efficiency, inventory turnover, and profitability. Using SQL, Python, and Power BI, the analysis identifies top-performing vendors, uncovers slow-moving inventory, and provides actionable recommendations to improve procurement strategies and maximize business profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Businesses often struggle with vendor dependency, excess inventory, and inconsistent profit margins. This project helps answer key business questions such as:

- Which vendors contribute the most to sales and purchases?
- Which brands generate high profit but low sales?
- How much capital is locked in unsold inventory?
- Does bulk purchasing reduce procurement costs?
- Which vendors require pricing or inventory optimization?

# 📈 Key Business Insights

✅ Top **10 vendors contributed 65.69%** of total purchases, highlighting dependency on a small group of suppliers.

✅ **198 brands** generated high profit margins but low sales, presenting opportunities for targeted marketing and pricing optimization.

✅ Vendors purchasing in bulk achieved approximately **72% lower unit costs**, demonstrating the financial benefits of bulk procurement.

✅ Total **Unsold Inventory Capital reached $2.71M**, indicating excess stock that impacts cash flow and storage costs.

✅ Low-performing vendors recorded an average **41.55% profit margin**, while top-selling vendors averaged **31.17%**, suggesting higher margins do not always translate into higher sales.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

- Diversify vendor partnerships to reduce supply chain risks.
- Promote high-margin, low-sales brands through targeted marketing campaigns.
- Optimize purchase quantities to reduce unsold inventory.
- Expand bulk purchasing strategies where cost savings are significant.
- Review pricing strategies for low-performing vendors to improve sales volume without sacrificing profitability.
